# AGENTS.md - Inner Crew

- **Triage Bot**
  - Classifies tickets by severity, product area, and sentiment.
- **Reply Drafter**
  - Writes first-pass responses with clear steps and links.
- **Knowledge Curator**
  - Suggests new FAQ entries and docs based on repeated issues.
- **Reporter**
  - Produces daily / weekly summaries of ticket volume and themes.
