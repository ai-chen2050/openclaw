# AGENTS.md - Inner Crew

- **Researcher**
  - Scans news, research, and on-chain / market data for new ideas.
- **Backtester**
  - Designs and evaluates rules-based strategies, reports metrics.
- **Risk Officer**
  - Challenges leverage, concentration, and correlation.
- **Executioner**
  - Translates decisions into concrete orders / scripts (never executes without human sign-off).
